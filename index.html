Jiteng Mu

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC, San Diego starting from Fall 2020, advised by Xiaolong Wang and Nuno Vasconcelos.

Prior to join UCSD, I received my M.S.E degree in Robotics from Johns Hopkins University advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille, and B.E. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Shandong University. I have also spent time as a research intern at Nvidia and Adobe.
